 Description: Innocents at Risk is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit founded to fight child exploitation and human trafficking. With a mission to educate citizens about the grave issue of global and local human trafficking Innocents at Risk has been working to raise awareness about the horrors of child trafficking in America and around the world. The organization is dedicated to protecting children from all forms of abuse, and work to end child exploitation and child trafficking everywhere. The organization conducts educational outreach programs through churches, clubs, organizations, schools and embassies.

Additionally, Innocents at Risk has an awareness DVD that has been used at every major embassy and throughout the United States as an educational resource.
