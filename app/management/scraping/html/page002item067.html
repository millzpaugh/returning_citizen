 Description: The Community for Creative Nonviolence(CCNV) strives to ensure that the rights of the homeless and poor are not infringed upon, and that every person has access to life’s basic essentials – food, shelter, clothing and medical care. The CCNV is also committed to protecting the rights of the homeless, advocating on behalf of the undeserved and preparing homeless men and women to re-enter into mainstream society as skilled and productive citizens.

The 1,350-Bed Federal City Shelter is the largest and most comprehensive facility of its kind in America. They also provide clothing to residents. Donors, including stores, contribute thousands of pounds of clothing and shoes to CCNV's residents each year. Surplus clothing is sold in The Treasure Chest Thrift Store when so designated by donors.
