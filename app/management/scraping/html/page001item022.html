 Description: Building Futures’ mission is to increase access to safe, affordable quality housing for families, children and individuals living with HIV/AIDS in Washington, DC. This provides a foundation for building dignified, stable, and healthy lives and futures.

HOUSING Building Futures is a special needs housing programs for families living with HIV/AIDS and other disabilities. Building Futures offers agency owned family apartment units, subsidized rental assistance in privately owned apartments, and short-term financial assistance for help with mortgages or rents, utilities, and basic telephone services. Residents pay 30% rent of the family income. Apply directly to Building Futures--applications are also available on the website. There is often a waiting list for placement.

SOCIAL SERVICES Building Futures offers medical case management services and weekly peer support groups open to residents of their housing program and the broader community. Building Futures offer support groups supporting the following targeted groups: (1) Persons living with HIV/AIDS who are in recovery from alcohol or substance abuse and (2) persons living with HIV/AIDS focused on physical and mental health issues helping participants live with the disease.
