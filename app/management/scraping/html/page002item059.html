Description: The Columbia Lighthouse for the Blind offers services for both the severely vision-impaired and for those with low vision. Services offered for individuals who are severely vision-impaired include: rehabilitation training, typing and computer skills training, vocational evaluations, job-site analysis, daily living skills, a sheltered workshop and general social services. Those who may have low vision may receive vision evaluations. There is also what is referred to as leisure-time and provisions for children's services. There is no age requirement to receive services.
