 Description: The D.C. Employment Justice Center (EJC) strives to attain a mission that helps to secure, protect and promote workplace justice in the D.C. metropolitan area. The EJC has successfully used a combination of strategies to protect the rights of low-income workers, including legal services, policy advocacy, community organizing, and education.

The DC Employment Justice Center provides legal assistance and consultations regarding employment law matters. Cases are generally handled by law students under the supervision of a licensed attorney. The Workers’ Rights Clinics are held at both Bread for the City locations, 1524 7th Street, NW on Wednesdays from 6:00 pm to 8:00 pm and 1640 Good Hope Road, SE 20020 Mondays from 3:30 pm to 4:30 pm.
