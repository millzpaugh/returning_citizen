HOUSING
ERCPCP provides 6-month transitional housing for returning prisoners referred by CSOSA or the US District Court. The transitional housing address and contact information is: 4115 First Street, SE. (Main Line) (202) 583-3890 (Fax) (202) 583-3693

SOCIAL SERVICES
ERCPCP offers case management, life skills education, financial management, and job search assistance. Participant must work or receive regular support. Rent is $125 per week plus a requirement to contribute to a personal escrow account which is returned upon their completion of the program.
