 Description: Living Wages is a community based, non-profit organization that provides adult education in Southeast Washington, DC. Living Wages operates two community-based adult education centers in Southeast Washington, D.C. Both centers are in an area of the city with very few educational opportunities. Living Wages runs a Basic Literacy and GED Preparation program, an External Diploma Program and offers computer literacy training and seminars for personal and professional development.

BASIC LITERACY & GED PREPARATION
The Basic Literacy and GED Preparation programs help to prepare individuals for the GED Exam, improve math, reading and writing skills, study science, social studies and literature, as well as provisions for participants to take the GED practice test.
Requirements for enrollment: To participate in our program, you must be 18 years of age or older and have DC residency. There is a $10 per month fee, and students may purchase GED books.

Open Enrollment: Call (202) 574-3961 Hours: Mon. – Fri. 9 a.m. to 4 p.m.

EXTERNAL DIPLOMA PROGRAM (EDP)
EDP is for motivated, self-directed adults 25 years or older who are DC residents. Students earn a Ballou STAY High School Diploma, while working on their own schedule and pace, learning; reading, writing, math and computer skills. The program takes 6 months to 2 years to complete, and has a pay as you go fee of $130 for materials.

Open Enrollment: Call (202) 574-3962 Hours: Mon. – Fri. 9 a.m. – 4 p.m. Sat. 10 a.m. - 2 p.m.
