Services provided at Adam’s house meet both individual and group needs as follows: Individual Services include: counseling, parent outreach, child support advocacy, HIV/AIDS testing, birth control, STI (Sexually transmitted infections) testing and treatment, health assessments, sports physicals, health education, job preparation, family court services, crisis intervention, and anger management.

Group Services include: male involvement, fatherhood development, motherhood development, parenting, anger management, family counseling, youth group sessions, domestic violence counseling, and family court services.
