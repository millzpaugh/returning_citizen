 Description: The Coalition for the Homeless (Employment Services Center) provides job-readiness training and a work-to-bed program. The Job-readiness training program is for homeless shelter men. The program provides participants with case management, substance abuse counseling, and resume preparation. Additionally, the program helps participants with interviewing skills, life skills, and employment referrals for homeless individuals or those on the verge of becoming homeless.

The Work-to-Bed program accepts applicants who are 18 yrs + that are employed (at least 20 hours per week) and can provide 3 paystubs, a letter from employer, recent TB results and a police clearance.
