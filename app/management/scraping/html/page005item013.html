 Description: HYPER Youth Treatment Program (Helping Young People Perform, Excel and Recover). Hyper provides services to parents and families in conjunction with Federal City Recovery Services to promote wellness of youth in the District. Federal City Recovery Services (FCRS) has developed a highly effective youth treatment program, driven via a performance based and positive outcomes model that effectively assists youth and their families arrest, prevent and/or reduce chronic substance abuse and criminal behavior.

The HYPPER Youth Treatment continuum of care includes a customized treatment regimen—addressing all aspects of an individual's life, including mental health services—and follow–up options with community and family-based recovery support systems that are crucial to a young person's success in achieving and maintaining a drug–free lifestyle. FCRS provides age appropriate Youth Substance Treatment Programming that focuses on co-occurring disorders. The program is designed to encourage and help youth develop social skills that will better equip them to make balanced and healthy decisions, motivate critical thinking skills to deter them from at-risk behavior(s) that lead to chronic drug addiction and criminal behaviors.
