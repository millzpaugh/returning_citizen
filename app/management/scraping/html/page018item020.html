 Description: Sasha Bruce YouthBuild is vocational training and placement assistance program for Department of Youth Rehabilitation Services(DYRS) committed youth (16-21 yrs). This is a one-year program, for unemployed youth who are interested in developing skills in construction trades (primarily carpentry). Applicants must be in need of a GED and read on at least 6th grade level (only - 4 slots for post GED applicants). The course curriculum rotates with 2 weeks of classroom participation and 2 weeks at the job site. Program participants will receive a weekly stipends of $75 for classroom and $6.15 per hour for job site participation.

The program also offers substance abuse counseling, peer support groups, GED participation, job placement and college counseling. Applicants must have birth certificate, (2) proofs of DC residency, police clearance, DC library card, picture ID. Mon-Fri 8:15 am to 3:30 pm
