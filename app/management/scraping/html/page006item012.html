 Description: Community of Hope has provided hope and stability to low-income and homeless adults and children in DC. It includes a holistic range of programs - from healthcare to housing with supportive services and programs promoting strong families - helps underserved residents create stable lives for themselves and promising futures for their children.

Community of Hope operates two temporary housing programs that house homeless families with children. Program works with the families to help them find affordable housing, increase their income, and work on self-identified goals to strengthen their families.

The Girard Street program provides, apartment-style, temporary shelter for 20 homeless families with children. Supportive services include a focus on helping families move quickly out of homelessness to stable housing, referrals to job training, mental health providers, and substance abuse treatment, and life skills classes on parenting, budgeting, and more. Girard Street Apartments is located in the Columbia Heights neighborhood of Northwest DC.
