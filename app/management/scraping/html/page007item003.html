 Description: SOME provides comprehensive addiction recovery services to homeless men and women who are ready, with the support of SOME staff, to break the cycle of addiction and homelessness. The Harvest House is a transitional housing program for women (children cannot be in the program) in substance abuse recovery. Harvest House provides relapse prevention, job training, job search assistance. Harvest House requires AA/NA participation. They accept referrals through other residential drug programs, i.e. 2nd Genesis, RAP, Inc. The average stay is 120 days.
