 Description: Unity Health Care Homeless Service Centers have been providing homeless services since 1985. Currently, the program operates 10 homeless medical service sites located in almost every ward of the District of Columbia. Unity provides quality, compassionate and comprehensive primary health care services to homeless individuals and families annually. The services provided by Unity Health Care’s extensive network allow assistance throughout the community including sites located in emergency shelters and homeless service facilities across the city.

The medical site at Christ House expands services to a 34-bed temporary residence and health care facility which has been providing hope and healing for 25 years for the sick and homeless of Washington, DC. Services include: Primary medical care for adult men and women, HIV Testing, Treatment, & Case Management, Psychiatric Services, Social Service, Substance Abuse referrals, and TB Screenings.
