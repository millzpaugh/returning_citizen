Eligibility Requirements include the following:
• Enrolled in or just graduating from a high school in the D.C. area;
• 16 years old or older;
• Willing to work outdoors in all weather conditions with a variety of landscaping tools and equipment;
• Interested in caring for and learning about trees; and
• Ability to lift and carry 40 lbs.
