 Description: Bethesda Cares, Inc. offers flexible, creative responses to problems related to homelessness and poverty. Services are offered to those living on the street from the DC Metro area. Homeless prevention is available for residents of Montgomery County. A high priority of Bethesda Cares in stopping the creation of homeless people and the organization works hard in their prevention efforts. The program offers Eviction prevention, Outreach Specialist, a Meal Program, clothing closet program, assistance with prescriptions and psychiatric services.

EVICTION PREVENTION PROGRAM:
Bethesda Cares works with multiple agencies to coordinate housing and services for clients. The program also takes care to engage clients and gain trust on multiple levels so clients can feel comfortable being linked into multiple services.

ADDITIONAL SERVICES:
An Outreach Specialist is available Monday through Friday for discussion and referrals. The Outreach Specialist meets the clients where they are located. Sometimes clients are located on the streets, and sometimes the clients come to our Drop-In Center. The Outreach Specialist fits the needs of the client to the services to form a strategy for each client to exit homelessness. This often requires housing placement and networking with housing providers.

Food for the hungry is available every day of the week through at four area churches that participate in the lunch program. Locations and times may be found on their website.

A Clothing Closet is available to provide clean donated clothing to clients.

Assistance with Prescriptions is available for homeless clients only.

Psychiatric Services are available onsite one morning a week for homeless clients.

VOLUNTEER OPPORTUNITIES
Volunteers are needed to help serve meals, sort and distribute clothing.
