 Description: Affiliated Community Counselors (ACCI) is a private, non-profit mental health clinic that primarily serves children and their families in the greater Rockville area. Their goal is to create a supportive environment for clients to explore their emotional needs and overcome barriers that limit personal growth and healing. They offer individual, group, family, and couples therapy and cover a wide range of topics including: depression and sadness; anger and frustration; anxiety and stress; communication issues; trust and relationship issues; sex and emotional intimacy issues; addictions; trauma and abuse; self-esteem issues; and career counseling. They service special populations to include seniors over age 55, persons in the LGBT community, persons with intellectual disability, and persons with physical disability. ACCI accepts public funding, self-payment, sliding scale fee, Medicaid, Medicare, and most private health insurances.
