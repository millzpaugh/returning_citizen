 Description: Guest House dedicates itself to the empowerment of female ex-offenders who desire to create a better life for themselves after incarceration. Guest House also provides a temporary home for women who need vital, basic support services so they can start a new life and transition back into society and lead successful lives.

Housing, food, counseling, employment assistance, medical, legal and social services are provided to clients so they can become self-sufficient and responsible members of the community.

Guest House accepts female ex-offenders with at least one year left on probation or parole. The Guest House is a 90-day residential program with case management services, life skills education, and peer drug counseling. Guest House considers referrals from social services agencies, treatment programs and/or Department of Corrections case managers.
