 Description: Capital Area Immigrants’ Rights (CAIR) Coalition is the only non-profit organization in the Washington, D.C. area with a legal services program focused exclusively on assisting immigrants detained by Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) in local jails in Virginia and the western part of Maryland. CAIR Coalition also provides individuals and organizations with education, training, and legal support services in efforts to increase the number of pro bono attorneys trained to represent detained immigrants who seek asylum or some other type of immigrant relief. Lastly, the CAIR Coalition advocates for the rights of both detained and non-detained immigrants in collaboration with, and on behalf of, the Coalition partners.
