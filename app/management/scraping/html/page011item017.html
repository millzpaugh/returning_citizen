 Description: Loaves and Fishes, a ministry of St. Stephen and the Incarnation Episcopal Church, has served lunch to the hungry and homeless on Saturdays, Sundays and federal holidays since 1968. Since most Washington food programs serve only on weekdays, the program offers a crucial service to its guests, some of whom travel across the city to be able to eat on the weekend. The program serves the Mt. Pleasant community in Washington, D.C. and is supported by a number of churches and volunteer organizations from around the D.C. metro area.

Loaves & Fishes offers hot meals at the St. Stephen’s Church on Saturdays, Sundays, and Mondays at 12 noon. Nearest Metro Station: Columbia Heights Metro Station.

VOLUNTEER OPPORTUNITIES
Volunteers are needed to prepare meals, clean kitchen, and serve food. (Spanish speakers are strongly encouraged to volunteer.) Accepts court ordered community service.
