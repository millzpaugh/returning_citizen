 Description: Melwood is a dynamic nonprofit that creates jobs and opportunities to improve the lives of people with disabilities. Melwood serves more than 2,400 people with disabilities in the greater Washington, DC area; and offers job opportunities for more than 1,200 people with disabilities through partnerships with the AbilityOne Program and community employers. Melwood supports opportunities for people with disabilities to maximize their independence and inclusion in the community through the provision of individualized programs that provide the necessary support to help individuals remain at home with their families, or to live as independently as possible in their own homes. Services are based on one’s individual needs. The Individual Support Services (ISS) and Family Support Services (FSS) offered through Melwood include: housing assistance; parenting; assisting with medical appointments, recreational and social opportunities; and independent living training.
