Description: Unity Health Care Homeless Service Centers have been providing homeless services since 1985. Currently, the program operates 10 homeless medical service sites located in almost every ward of the District of Columbia. Unity provides quality, compassionate and comprehensive primary health care services to homeless individuals and families annually. The services provided by Unity Health Care’s extensive network allows assistance throughout the community including sites located in emergency shelters and homeless service facilities across the city.

Our newest clinic at Pathways represents and important collaboration between Unity Health Care and Pathways to Housing to provide medical care for our homeless patients who are actively engaged in the process of becoming housed. Only primary care services are offered at this site.
