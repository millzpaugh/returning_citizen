 Description: Unity Health Care Homeless Service Centers have been providing homeless services since 1985. Currently, the program operates 10 homeless medical service sites located in almost every ward of the District of Columbia. Unity provides quality, compassionate and comprehensive primary health care services to homeless individuals and families annually. The services provided by Unity Health Care’s extensive network allow assistance throughout the community including sites located in emergency shelters and homeless service facilities across the city.

The Federal City Health Center expands our services to the CCNV shelter which has capacity of 1,350 beds and has been serving the City for over 30 years. Federal City is the largest and most comprehensive facility of its kind in America.

Services include: Primary medical care for adult men and women, HIV Testing/Treatment, Case Management, Psychiatric Services, Social Service, Substance Abuse counseling & referrals, Diabetic Education, Dental, and Laboratory Services. The Federal City Health Center also provides Specialty Care services which include: Urology, Nephrology, Podiatry, Orthopedic, ENT, TB Screenings , Infectious Disease , and GYN.
