 Description: The EXCEL Institute offers participants (16 yrs +) training in a 2 year automotive technology training program. The program, additionally, offers follow-up job placement assistance for participants with at least the 9th grade academic skills. Participants will also have access to on-site GED preparation. Students earn a “Certificate of Achievement” upon completion of each course, (i.e. electrical systems, fuel systems, auto body) and many students find employment, in the automotive, field prior to completing the 2 year curriculum.

Enrollment opens 3 times per year (Sept., Jan., & June). Classes are held year-round, from Monday – Friday, beginning at 8:45 am and ending at 1:30 pm or 5:30 pm to 8:30 pm. The GED classes offered through the program begin at 1:30 pm and end at 2:30 pm. Students will also be required to prepare to pass ASE (Automotive Service Excellence) examination. No stipends are offered through the program.
