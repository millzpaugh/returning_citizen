 Description: The Welcome Home Program is an aid to men and women returning home to Washington D.C., Montgomery County and Prince George's County after being released from incarceration. Most of these individuals will face a variety of barriers and have great difficulty managing the most basic necessities for successful reintegration-reconnecting with jobs, housing, and their families, as well as gaining access to needed substance abuse and health care treatment. To help assist in the navigation of services Prison Ministries offers assistance that include assistance with barriers to employment, burnt bridges with family/friends, difficulty acquiring housing, lack of support and encouragement and overall navigation in the “free world.”
