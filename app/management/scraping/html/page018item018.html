 Description: A SANE nurse is a Sexual Assault Nurse Examiner. These specialty nurses provide nursing care, medical testing and support services to adult victims of rape, sexual assault, and other sex crimes. Within 96 hours (4 days) of the assault, victim should go to the SANE Program at Washington Hospital Center (WHC) Emergency room for medical services, testing, and referrals.

Survivors are offered HIV testing at WHC. If not HIV positive, survivors are eligible to take HIV prophylaxis (post-exposure) medication for 28 days to reduce chances of contracting HIV as a result of the assault. No cost.
