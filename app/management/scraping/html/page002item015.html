 Description: Capitol Hill Group Ministry’s provides transitional housing and permanent housing through two programs, the Systems Transformation Initiative (STI) and Shelter Plus Care (SPC).

SYSTEMS TRANSFORMATION INITIATIVE:
Systems Transformation Initiative (STI), provides families preparing to live independently in the middle stage between emergency and permanent shelters with apartment-style housing and individualized resources. Families in the 2-year intensive STI program are supported through holistic case management, monthly meetings, life skills workshops, and referrals, as well as assistance with budgets, groceries, and transportation in order to improve their quality of life and move towards self-reliance. Contact LahTisha Miller at (202) 548-4012.

SHELTER PLUS CARE (SPC)
Capitol Hill Group Ministry's Permanent Housing program provides shelter and supportive services on a long-term basis for homeless neighbors living with dual diagnosis. Ongoing services are provided so that individuals may still have the opportunity to experience stability, and lead healthy lives. Services are provided for referrals to programs including employment, education, child care, substance abuse treatment, mental health support, and legal assistance. Contact Hakima Cheik at (202) 543-2929.
