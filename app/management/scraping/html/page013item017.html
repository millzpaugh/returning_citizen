 Description: New Course Restaurant & Catering offers an 18-week culinary arts and food preparation course with hands-on training at 2 locations. Training locations can be found at New Course Restaurant and Catering, 500 3rd Street, NW and the US Tax Court, dining room, 400 2nd Street, NW. Trainees will learn food preparation, food storage, serving techniques; work place ethnics and responsibilities. Trainees will also receive free meals; a Chef’s Jacket; a weekly stipend ($60); and a DC Food Handler’s License upon graduation.

Training hours are Monday -Friday, from 6:00 am to 3:00 pm, (TANF trainees schedule is from 9:00 am to 3:00 pm). Classes start every other month and applicants must complete a one-day trial period to assess work ethic and aptitude. Please call for available dates for screening.

Eligibility requirements include: High School diploma or GED, drug free for six months or currently active in a recovery program, and able to fulfill the time obligations of the program.
