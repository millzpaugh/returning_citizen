 Eligibility Criteria: Homeless mothers (w/ up to 3 children). All are welcome for food and clothing.
 Documentation Needed: No ID required.
 Description: The Dorothy Day Catholic Worker Community House serves as a food distribution and clothing service location, on Wednesdays from 9:00 am to 11:30 am, to the homeless. Through the housing program, transitional housing for women with children is available with services of case manager (i.e. referrals for other agency programs and/or day care). Potential residents may stay at least 1 year in the facility. All residents must either be in school or working 20 hours a week (saving 30% of all income earned).

Homeless families (with up to 3 children) are the targeted demographic. Admission and house operation is governed by the consensus of the current residents. The house can accommodate up to 5 families. Means are provided when donated, food is available; laundry machines available; and there are no TV's (nor may any potential resident bring TV's) and no spanking children.

The nearest Metro station to the facility is Georgia Avenue/Petworth; or Metro bus: 70/79, 62/63, and H8. NOTE: At 9:00 am the wait-list opens up and names are placed on the wait-list.
