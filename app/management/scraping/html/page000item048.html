 Hours Of Services: 7am-6pm
 Eligibility Criteria: District resident without private medical insurance or whose medical insurance does not cover substance abuse treatment services
 Documentation Needed: Proof of residency
 Description: APRA is the single state authority for substance abuse services for the District of Columbia for residents who do not have insurance, have DC Medicaid, or whose insurance does not cover substance abuse treatment services. APRA administers the Drug Treatment Choice Program which contracts with approximately 30 treatment programs that provide services such as detoxification, outpatient, intensive outpatient, and residential drug treatment. Adults (21+) access treatment through the Assessment and Referral Center (The ARC) at 70 N Street, NE or the satellite office at 500 Indiana Ave., NW. Youth access treatment from 4 different locations: Federal City Recovery Services, Hillcrest Children’s Center, Latin American Youth Center, or Riverside Treatment Center. Youth can contact these centers directly for services. APRA also receives special funding (Access to Recovery / ATR) to provide an extended continuum of Recovery Support Services. These services include: Recovery support evaluation, Care coordination services, Recovery coaching & mentoring, Education support services (individual & group), Transportation (public only), and Environmental stability.
