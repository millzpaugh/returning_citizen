EMERGENCY FOOD
Emmaus Services for the Aging provides access to some basic food supplies for older adults 55+ on a need basis; this need is determined by case by case evaluation done by the Agency. Emmaus also offers a range of programmatic services which include opportunities for educational and technology; access to health and wellness programs and discussions such as Thrive & Jive and Raise the Bar exercise programs. We also provide a nutritious and well balanced lunch which is served to all active participants. Additionally, Emmaus delivers food supplies to eligible older adults, 60+ who are enrolled in the program. This is usually done on the 3rd Saturday of the month with the help of Volunteers.
