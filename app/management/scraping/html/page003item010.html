 Description: This service is the gatekeeper for recognized shelters in DC (adult and family). The United Planning Organization ("UPO") manages hourly tally of available beds. This referral service will attempt to place residents according to their needs. This service operates hypothermia mobile vans that respond to individuals and families in immediate need of shelter.

UPO provides scheduled transportation for shelter residents during hypothermia season, beginning November 1st and ending March 31st. The service transports residents who spent the night in a church based facility where residents must exit the shelter by 7am and encouraged to go to day programs where they can receive shelter and services during the daytime hours. The day programs provide food, comfort and other life skills support. See www.ich.dc.gov for shelter pick-up and drop-off locations.
