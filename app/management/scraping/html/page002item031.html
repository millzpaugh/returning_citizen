 Description: Centro Tepeyac Silver Spring Women's Center (Centro Tepeyac) is a nonprofit Catholic agency, serving greater Washington, DC area women and men in pregnancy-related crises and offering them a meaningful alternative to abortion. Although the center was founded to provide assistance primarily to Hispanic women and their families, it serves all persons in need. At Centro Tepeyac, counseling is gentle, charitable, compassionate, truthful and informative. Following the example of Christ’s love, Centro Tepeyac seeks to meet the emotional, physical and spiritual needs, for enabling and encouraging women to choose life. The clinic provides pregnancy testing, referral services, confidential counseling, sexual integrity education, parenting information and post-abortion guidance. All services are free and confidential and available in Spanish and English.

Practical Services include the following: Bilingual advice, Ongoing support, during pregnancy, childbirth and after birth; with parents, boyfriend or husband, as needed; Prenatal & Parenting Classes, support groups and child-care programs, Maternity and baby clothing, furniture and supplies, Post abortion counseling & peer support, and Instruction in natural family planning.

If you have questions or wish further information about our programs, or just wish to talk to one of our counselors, please call us at (301) 587-9516, or email us at info@centrotepeyac.org.
