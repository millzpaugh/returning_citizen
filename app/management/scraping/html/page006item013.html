 Description: The Golden Triangle Business Improvement District (BID) is a private non-profit 501(c)(6) corporation that works to enhance the Golden Triangle neighborhood—a 43-block area of DC’s central business district that is home to global corporations, law firms, non-profits, associations and also bustling restaurants, hotels, and boutiques. The BID's primary focus is to provide a clean, safe and vibrant environment within this remarkable neighborhood for area workers and visitors. One of the ways this is accomplished is through its Homeless Outreach program.

The Homeless Outreach Program helps homeless persons move from life on the streets to a life of independence, good health and stability. The BID contracts outreach workers to develop relationships with homeless people to provide for their basic needs and to link them with services that help get them off the street. Services range from providing bus tokens for job hunting to assisting homeless persons with enrolling in mental health and substance abuse treatment programs.
