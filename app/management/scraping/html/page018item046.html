 Description: The St. Columba's Episcopal Church's Water Ministry provides lunch, showers and laundry facilities for people who are homeless or in need. It is facilitated by volunteers under the leadership of a paid director. It offers services on Monday, Tuesday, Thursday and Friday from 11:00 am to 1:00 pm, early September through early June. Volunteers contribute two or more hours per month working as a team to host 30 to 40 guests each day.
Volunteer training opportunities are offered.
Nearest Metro station: Tenleytown

Weekend Meals on Wheels: The Water Ministry delivers nutritious sustenance to ease the isolation of an elderly shut-in by delivering a meal. Deliveries are scheduled for the second Saturday of each month from 10:00 am to 12:00 noon. Willingness to drive is helpful, but not required. Weekend Meals on Wheels is a family-friendly ministry; children are welcome to accompany you.
