 Eligibility Criteria: Families with children (18 +); Children (17 and under); Must be 30% or below the AMI.
 Description: Hope and a Home is a transitional housing program for homeless families (must have children). Call-ins are accepted from both individuals and agencies, but walk-ins are not accepted. Required case management support includes: goal setting, budget planning, academic tutoring (children), educational advocacy, employment assistance and job retention counseling. Agency referrals and call-in's are accepted.

Referrals are accepted from the Virginia Williams Center, but families are not required to be registered with the Virginia Williams Center to be considered for placement. There is a three year maximum stay. All potential clients must meet income eligibility requirements.

NOTE: Hope and a Home is not an emergency shelter and is not able to provide emergency services.
