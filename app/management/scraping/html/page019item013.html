 Description: Green Door provides comprehensive programs designed to meet the needs of people with mental illness including: Psychiatric & Support Services - Green Door is certified by the Department of Mental Health as a Core Service Agency (CSA) and provides outpatient mental health services to include: assessment, psychiatric services, medication management, health education, case management by community support workers, individual and group counseling, community support, 24 hour crisis intervention, co-occurring disorders groups, and assistance for clients involved in the criminal justice system (i.e. Jail Linkage program), Assertive Community Treatment (ACT).

NEXT STEP PROGRAM
Intensive goal-driven day program focusing on behavioral management, information and discussion about medication and their side effects, reading and comprehension, coping techniques and exercise.

EDUCATION SERVICES:
The Green Door Education Services staff provides support to clients who want to learn basic reading and/or math skills, continue their education and earn their GED, high school diploma, college degree, or vocational certification. Assistance includes assessment testing for off- site education programs; identifying an appropriate literacy organization, vocational program, or educational institution that would best fit client’s educational goals; and helping to explore possible funding for tuition, transportation and supplies.

EMPLOYMENT SERVICES:
Green Door is a participant in the Ticket to Work Program, an initiative of the Social Security Administration that encourages individuals who receive Social Security Disability Insurance or Supplemental Security Income to seek and find employment. They also provide clients with counseling and education needed to obtain employment such as resume writing, how to begin the job search process, how to get along with co-workers, how employment affects SSI benefits, etc. Housing Services - Green Door provides affordable housing for people with severe mental illness whether it is independent living in a home or apartment, or living in a group home environment.

Green Door is publicly funded through DMH and accepts, Medicaid, Medicare, and state financed insurance (i.e. chartered health, unison, etc.).
