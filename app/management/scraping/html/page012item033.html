 Description: Montgomery County Family Center strives to build partnerships within the community to provide services, education, support and promote self-sufficiency and empowerment. The Center welcomes all Montgomery County residents to walk-in to get access to services. The Center offers services in the following areas: case management, crisis intervention and referral services; resume and job application assistance; nutrition, cooking and exercise classes; food distribution; clothing and shoes shopping day; parent education classes; financial education classes; English as a second language classes; rent, mortgage and utility assistance; immigration legal services; and services for pregnant women.

SERVICE DETAILS:
CASE MANAGEMENT & REFERRALS (crisis intervention also): Walk-in hours are Monday – Friday from 8:00am – 4:30 pm.

EDUCATION: Beginner & Intermediate English Class (ESL): Tuesday from 6:00 pm – 8:00 pm

MONEY SMART: FINANCIAL EDUCATION CLASSES
Classes are taught on budgeting, saving and banking, please call for dates and information regarding registration. Classes are typically held on Tuesdays from 6:00 pm – 8:30 pm. Employment: Resume and job application assistance, including resume writing and job appointments.

FAMILY:
Parent Support Class (Spanish) is offered on Thursdays from 12:00 pm – 1:00 pm and childcare is provided with snacks and activities.

FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE:
The Neighborhood Service center offers Department of Health and Human Services through walk-ins to complete applications for income assistance programs (Temporary Cash Assistanc, Food Stamps, Medical Assistance, Childcare subsidy program, eviction prevention program, utility disconnections and home energy program). Services are available every Wednesday and Friday from 9:00 am – 4:00 pm.

LEGAL SERVICES:
Legal Services are provided for immigration needs every Thursday at 9:00am. There is a $60 consultation fee associated with this service (cash or check only). Additionally, the Archdiocese Legal Network provides services for free to low-income residents. Intake is open Monday – Friday from 9:30 am – 12:00 pm and 2:00 pm – 4:30 pm). Call for more information (202) 628-4263.
