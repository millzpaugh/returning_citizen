 Eligibility Criteria: Decease’s liquid assets cannot exceed $800 (including banking accounts, social security and final paychecks); DC residents
 Description: The District of Columbia's Burial Assistance Program provides up to $800 toward burial or $450 toward cremation for DC residents. Burial or cremation services must be arranged through a funeral home under contract with DC Government.

To apply go to 645 H Street, NE (Income Maintenance Administration (IMA)) with documents establishing relationship to deceased i.e. marriage certificate, death certificate, income and asset verification), proof of decease’s DC residency. DC residents who died with a HIV or AIDS related illness can receive additional burial assistance benefits through a program administered by Samaritan Ministry (202) 889-7702 ext 306.
