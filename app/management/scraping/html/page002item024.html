EDUCATION/EMPLOYMENT-VOCATIONAL
Catholic Charities offers adult education classes. They offer ESL classes for Spanish speaking persons for 8 weeks at a cost of $100. They also offer (1) GED preparation (on-going classes, day and evenings), (2) computer training (Mon, Wed, Thurs 6:00 pm - 8:00 pm), (3) building maintenance classes (classes are taught in Spanish), and (4)job readiness weekend classes.

LEGAL
Catholic Charities also provide legal assistance and representation for child custody matters, consumer debt/bankruptcy, domestic violence, employment disputes, guardianship cases, landlord-tenant disputes, public benefits, and wills. Appointment line (202-628-4263) Mon-Fri 9:30 am - 12:30 pm. Free to low fee.

Catholic Charities also offers legal assistance with immigration issues. They offer legal assistance for persons in detention, removal hearings, work authorization, asylum applications, family visas, work visas, BIA appeals, federal court appeals, and community education. They also provided legal assistance for naturalization issues such as helping complete forms, applications, and appeals. There is a flat fee of $60 for each consultation. Fees can be waived with approval.
