 Description: Charlie’s Place uses its outreach ministry services to provide job placement, housing placement, outreach to street dwelling homeless, HIV/AIDS testing and counseling and bag lunches. Services offered at Charlie’s Place are as follows:

Morning Meal – We serve a hot nutritious meal every Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday and Friday to approximately 60 – 80 clients.

Case Management – Our main focus through case management is job placement and housing placement. Since we expanded to include case management we have placed 112 clients into new employment and helped place 46 into permanent or transitional housing.

Lunch To-Go - Two days a week, we send our clients home with a bag lunch. Our lunches typically consist of a sandwich, piece of fruit and bottle of water to help sustain them throughout the day.

HIV Testing and Counseling – due to support from Brother Help Thyself, we are now contracted with Transgender Health Empowerment Inc. to provide on-site HIV/AIDS testing, counseling and education every two weeks. Since its conception, 622 Charlie’s Place clients have received HIV/AIDS testing and counseling.

Clothing Distribution - Donations from the community stock our clothing closet with everything from dress shirts suitable for a job interview, to jackets and sweaters for warmth, down to T-shirts, socks and underwear.
