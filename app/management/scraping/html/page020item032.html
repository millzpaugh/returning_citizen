 Description: Unity Health Care Homeless Service Centers have been providing homeless services since 1985. Currently, the program operates 10 homeless medical service sites located in almost every ward of the District of Columbia. Unity provides quality, compassionate and comprehensive primary health care services to homeless individuals and families annually. The medical site at Harbor Light expands services to Washington, DC’s 136-bed Salvation Army Harbor Light Center which provides six-month residential treatment primarily for men and women struggling with drug and alcohol addiction.
