 Description: Capital Community Services (CCS) provides an array of outpatient services and programs for adults 18 years and older including: diagnostic assessment, medication somatic treatment, individual and group counseling and psychotherapy, community support, and Assertive Community Treatment (ACT). Services are provided by qualified, licensed, and credentialed staff including: Board Certified Psychiatrists, Registered Nurses, Licensed Independent Clinical Social Workers, Certified Addictions Counselors, Licensed Professional Counselors, and Credentialed Mental Health Specialist. CCS is also certified as a Core Service Agency by the District of Columbia Department of Mental Health. This certification allows CCS to provide mental health rehabilitative services (MHRS) to the District's population. CCS also provides after hours emergency on-call services for consumers enrolled in the program. CCS services are covered by Medicaid, Medicare, as well as, several HMO’s. Payment arrangements are worked out based on coverage and CCS provides a sliding scale fee.
