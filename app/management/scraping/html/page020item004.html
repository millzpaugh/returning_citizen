 Description: The United Cerebral Palsy of Washington offers services to adults with cerebral palsy and other disabilities. The program focus is to advance the independence, productivity and quality of life for individuals with disabilities and their families. UCP’s adult services include Experiences Unlimited, a day program that provides individuals with meaningful community participation. Additional services include, a Health Service, which is a curriculum designed to help maintain individuals at an optimum level of functioning; Saturday leisure service, Medicaid Waiver program (for Saturday), and Assistive Technology services.
