 Description: FAIR Girls prevents he exploitation of girls worldwide with empowerment and education. Through prevention education, compassionate care, and survivor inclusive advocacy, Fair Girls creates opportunities for girls to become, happy, healthy young women. FAIR Girls is able to provide advocacy and crisis intervention services for girls, ages 11-21 years old, who are victims of human trafficking or at risk of being sexually exploited. The agency collaborates with Covenant House and Sasha Bruce to address emergency needs such as food, shelter, and clothing. There are also provisions for individual and group counseling at a confidential location and counseling services can also be provided off-site. The program promotes self-efficacy of rescued girls by assisting with resume building, job applications, and/or college applications.

FAIR Girls also operates "Jewels Girls," an empowerment program that teaches participants to make jewelry. Participants come together to create unique jewelry while gaining access to therapy, new life skills, financial management skills and pathways toward a future free of poverty and violence. The jewelry is sold at various venues and participants keep 50% of the revenue. The remaining proceeds go toward purchasing new supplies and materials to sustain her program.

If you or someone you know needs help please call the Fair Girls 24/7 crisis hotline number: 1-855-900-FAIR (3247). Someone from FAIR Girls will answer or return your call immediately - day or night. If there is a life threatening emergency please dial 9-1-1 first.
