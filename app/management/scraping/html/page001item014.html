 Description: Boat People S.O.S.(BPSOS) assists boat people and other refugees from Vietnam to resettle in this country. BPSOS also assists victims of persecution, violence and exploitation in building their new life in America. BPSOS offers services that are crucial to the Vietnamese American community -- ranging from domestic violence prevention to citizenship education to small business development among others. Additionally, BPSOS has formed new civic institutions and built strong communities across America.
