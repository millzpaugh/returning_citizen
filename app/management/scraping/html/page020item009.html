 Description: Unity Health Care Health Centers have service sites located in every ward of the District of Columbia. The Student Centers provide quality, compassionate and comprehensive primary health care services to students attending the perspective HS where the centers are located. Students can access Unity Health Care services regardless of ability to pay. For those who can pay, a sliding scale based on family size and income is used to determine the appropriate amount. Unity Health Care accepts Medicaid, Medicare, Managed Care Organizations, DC Healthcare Alliance (DC Chartered Health Care Alliance and United Health Care Community Plan), and other private insurance plans.

The Ballou Student Health Center, operated by Unity Health Care, is located on the campus of Ballou Senior High School to service the student population of the school. It offers a full range of health care and supportive services year-round. Services include:
Physical examinations, Sports physicals, Immunizations, Chronic disease management, Acute illness diagnosis, Pregnancy Testing, Family Planning, Sexually transmitted disease testing, Substance abuse counseling, Mental health referrals, and Case management services.

This site location also offers a full range of dental services in a state of the art operatory.

Teens who are interested in services provided by Unity Health Care Centers can call the Teen Appointment line at (202) 469-4699. All teens calling in for an appointment must provide the following information for emergency purposes: address, phone number, date of birth and parent/guardian contact information (parents will not be contacted).
