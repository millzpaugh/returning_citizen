PREGNANCY CENTER:

Northwest Center's, Pregnancy Center is a community outreach program where trained counselors work with women and teens to identify, discuss, and solve the many challenges surrounding pregnancy and motherhood. Women may go to the Pregnancy Center for pregnancy testing. Should a woman receive a positive test result she may utilize educational information regarding all life-affirming choices available. The center also provides prenatal education, parenting education, material assistance, diapers, and formula.

Pregnancy Center: (202) 483-7008

MATERNITY HOME:

Northwest Center's, Maternity Home is a comprehensive transitional housing program that provides a stable home for up to 18 months for homeless pregnant women (18-30 years) and their children. Participants receive case management services on a weekly basis; life skills training and access to adequate health care and heal education; assistance with and referrals for quality day care; development of interpersonal communication skills; counseling on household budgeting, savings and financial management. The program provides live-in staff and case manager and participants are expected to adhere to a set of rules that instill the discipline necessary to take care of a family. Referrals and linkages with a variety of community-based agencies are also available.

Maternity Home: (202) 483-2857
