 Description: The Martin Luther King Jr. library offers free computer classes, on an ongoing basis throughout the year. Classes are advertised on a monthly basis, usually about one week before the first of the month. In order for the library program to accommodate the needs of morning, afternoon and evening attendees the class times vary from month to month.

Each class is a one-time workshop and is scheduled for two to three hours of instruction with hands-on training. For this reason, customers are encouraged to repeat a class several times, in order to strengthen their skills, before moving on to the next level of classes. The year-round schedule allows for this kind of repetition in the learning process.
