 Description: Abused Persons Program (APP) serves Montgomery County residents who are victims of intimate partner abuse or persons who have committed intimate partner abuse and want to stop abusing. Victims can speak to someone anytime day or night by calling the 24 hour crisis hotline. The program offers information and assistance in seeking legal protections available to victims of intimate partner violence. It provides emergency shelter for victims; individual and group counseling to victims of domestic violence including children exposed to domestic violence, and counseling for the abusers themselves. Couples recovering from abuse are referred to community partners for counseling as well. APP provides education to various community groups about ending the cycle of intimate partner violence or domestic violence. There are no fees for emergency services, but fees for other services are on an ability-to-pay basis. No one is denied services due to lack of funds.
