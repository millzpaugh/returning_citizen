 Description: Marshall Heights Community Development Organization offers services in employment and vocational training, education, social services and housing assistance.

EMPLOYMENT ASSISTANCE:
Men and women can utilize the career assessment process, as well as counseling services offered for both employment and education. Participants may also receive pre-employment skills training, job placement and monitoring. Job training referrals and GED classes are Monday - Thursday from 9:00 am am to 5:00 pm. Through the intake and orientation process individuals can receive a career assessment, employment and education counseling, pre-employment skills training, job placement and monitoring; emergency service; housing assistance; and individual and peer group counseling.

SOCIAL SERVICES:
Marshall Heights Community Development Organization also offers emergency service in housing assistance and for individual and/or peer group counseling. Should any person be seeking assistance with food they can receive help Monday through Friday from 9:00 am to 5:30 pm (must show ID; proof of residence & referral). Persons may also seek out crisis clothing and counseling service for Ward 7 residents.

Housing counseling referrals; rental assistance, utilities assistance and mortgage default assistance. Provides transitional housing assistance for the homeless must be working. Nearest
