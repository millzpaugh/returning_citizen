EMERGENCY FOOD
The Calvary Food Bank is an emergency food pantry that is open on Fridays from 10:00 am to 1:00 pm (by appointment only). A referral is required for all families and senior citizens to receive emergency food services.

CLOTHING BANK
The clothing bank is open from Monday – Friday, 10:00 am to 3:00 pm. Referrals are accepted from the WIC Program (by appointment). The nearest metro station is Rhode Island Avenue; Metro bus: P6 & D8.
