 Description: AMEN (Anacostia Mentoring and Employment Network), provides people with training that allows each individual to learn real-world job skills and assistance with job placement in diverse fields. For many of the men and women who participate, learning to be an employee can be a new experience, and the staff of Southeast Ministry (SEM) puts them on the right path.

"A-MEN" offers a 3-week job readiness program followed by one-on-one job placement, mentoring, and counseling services. Participants receive bus tokens Monday-Friday plus a $20 stipend on Friday. GED classes are held from 9:00 am - 12:30 pm, Monday-Friday. Job Readiness classes are 1:00 pm - 4:30 pm, Monday-Friday. Participants are screened for the "willingness to change their lives."
