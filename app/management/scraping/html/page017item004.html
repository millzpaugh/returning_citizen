The center serves the neighborhoods of Congress Heights, Washington Highlands; Henson's Creek; and Barry Farms in southeast Washington, DC.

EMPLOYMENT AND VOCATIONAL TRAINING
Their staff helps low-income DC residents with valuable workforce skills placing them on a pathway to employment. Customers can enroll in the following training/certification programs: (1) basic computer training, (2) Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS) Certification, and (3) Food Service Handler Training and Certification.

SOCIAL SERVICES
Assists DC residents in reaching their desired level of sufficiency by placing an emphasis on family strengthening. This Community Service Center helps eligible residents with rent/mortgage and utility assistance when available. They also offer employment assistance, case management, and financial counseling.

Intake is Mondays and Thursdays, 9:00 am - 1:00 pm. Potentially eligible residents can take Metro bus lines, W4 and A8, to get to the center.
