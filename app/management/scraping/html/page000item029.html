 Description: American Rescue Workers (ARW) runs the DC Corps 9-Month Christian Addiction program. The program is open to men and is a 9-month, spiritually-based, residential treatment counseling and work therapy program. Alcohol abuse must be the primary treatment issue.

The initial phase is a 45 day boot camp, which mandates participants to have no outside contact. Through the Industrial program participants must work either on-site or at one of ARW’s thrift stores. Assignments are based on expertise and program needs. On-site interviews are required. Lastly, applicants must have picture ID and $20 for urinalysis, unless directly referred from a detox program. They must also be physically healthy and able to lift 25 pounds.
