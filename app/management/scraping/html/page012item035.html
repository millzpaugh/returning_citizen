Description: Mt. Carmel House is a permanent women's supportive back-to-work housing program. The program provides case management, life skills, job preparation, and psychiatric services by appointment. In order to participate in the program women must have a social summary (assessment and progress report) from their referral source. Participants must also be drug/alcohol free for at least 30 days prior to admission, and enroll in aftercare program/outpatient program. If a participant has any type of mental illness she must demonstrate compliance in treatment.
