 Description: Carpenter’s Shelter’s comprehensive continuum of care offers continuous services, aiding the chronically homeless and shelter residents through their transition back into independent living. Carpenter's Shelter's strive to empower clients to address the issues, which led them to homelessness, and provide the tools necessary to achieve lasting independence. 90% of those who complete the program never return to homelessness again.

Carpenter's Shelter offers substance abuse and mental health counseling, case management, and job readiness training. Residents are required to either seek or maintain full time employment. The average stay is 6 months.

All potential clients should be aware that family referrals need to be obtained from Alexandria Department of Human Services. Individuals must schedule an appointment with Homeless Services Assessment Center (703.548.0452).
