 Description: The Washington Legal Clinic for the Homeless is composed of expert staff and network of volunteer attorneys who provide low barrier, comprehensive legal services at intake sites throughout the District of Columbia, and help our clients to access housing, shelter, and life-saving services.

The Washington Legal Clinic for the Homeless operates through provisions of representation for persons with low-income or no-income in all civil legal matters. Civil matters include: public benefits, housing, employment, immigration, etc.; impact litigation, as well as legislative and policy-related issues. The clinic provides advice and advocacy. The clinic has multiple locations and interviews are usually conducted at an individual’s designated shelters/soup kitchens. For more information concerning services provided by the Washington Legal Clinic for the Homeless please call and request addresses of designated shelters/soup kitchens.
