 Description: The Davis Memorial Goodwill Industries strives to attain their mission of helping to transform lives and communities through the power of education and employment. They believe in building a community where people are empowered to achieve their fullest potential and RISE to their highest level of personal success. The Davis Memorial Goodwill Industries provides vocational services and employment to individuals with disabilities and disadvantage. When individuals are ready to apply for jobs, Davis Memorial Goodwill Industries provides resources to help them conduct job searches, write resumes, and practice their interviewing skills.

Clients entering any programs work with workforce development professionals to identify their experiences, aspirations, and develop an individual career plan designed to enhance personal and professional growth. Career skills training, also known as job readiness, help clients understand workplace etiquette and employer expectations. Clients also learn how to conduct an effective job search and develop the skills required in their respective career fields.
