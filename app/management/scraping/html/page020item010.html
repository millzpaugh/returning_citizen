 Description: Unity Health Care Health Centers have service sites located in every ward of the District of Columbia. The Centers provide quality, compassionate and comprehensive primary health care services. DC residents can access Unity Health Care services regardless of ability to pay. For those who can pay, a sliding scale based on family size and income is used to determine the appropriate amount. Unity Health Care accepts Medicaid, Medicare, Managed Care Organizations, DC Healthcare Alliance (DC Chartered Health Care Alliance and United Health Care Community Plan), and other private insurance plans.

Teens who are interested in services provided by Unity Health Care Centers can call the Teen Appointment line at (202) 469-4699. All teens calling in for an appointment must provide the following information for emergency purposes: address, phone number, date of birth and parent/guardian contact information (parents will not be contacted).

The medical services provided at the Brentwood Square site include the following: Primary Medical Care, Adult Medicine, Family Medicine, and Pediatrics care. Additionally, this site provides services in specialty care which include the following: OB/GYN, Health Education, Podiatry, Laboratory Services, Social Services, Adolescent Services, and Case Management.
