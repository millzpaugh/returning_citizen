SUBSTANCE ABUSE PROGRAM
The Living Free Program introduces the philosophy of the 12 Step Programs to include AA (Alcholoics Anonymous) and NA (Narcotics Anonymous). The program, additionally offers a comprehensive substance abuse/dependence assessment prior to any treatment recommendations. The assessment includes a psycho-social evaluation, alcohol drug history and use of standardized substance abuse inventories to determine a final diagnosis. There is an adult program, a primary treatment group, education group, relapse group, and therapy for individuals, couples, and family’s.

MENTAL HEALTH PROGRAM
Living Free provides the highest quality mental health care for individuals, couples and families experiencing social, emotional and/or behavioral problems. The target population is male and female adults who have problems with mood disorders, anxiety and stress, crisis, co-dependency issues, compulsion disorders, adjustment disorders, marital conflict, parent-child conflict, family issues, low self-esteem, post traumatic stress and HIV issues.
