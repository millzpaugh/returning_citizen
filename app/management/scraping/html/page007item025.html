 Description: Community of Hope operates two temporary housing programs that house homeless families with children. Program works with the families to help them find affordable housing, increase their income, and work on self-identified goals to strengthen their families.

The Hope program provides apartment-style transitional housing for 10 homeless families with children where the head of household is in recovery from substance abuse. Adults attend outpatient substance abuse treatment programs while simultaneously working on finding a job and stable housing. This is the only program in DC where families remain intact while the head of household works on recovery. Hope Apartments is located in the Congress Heights neighborhood of Southeast DC.
