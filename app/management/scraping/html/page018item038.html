 Description: The Southeast Veterans Center provides basic medical and preventative care, health education, mental health services, addiction counseling, comprehensive women's health care, nutritional counseling to eligible veterans, and referrals for persons who require specialized services.

Through the center veterans can seek transitional housing, Singe Room Occupancy housing (SRO) and access the drop-in center (eligible veterans). While being a part of the housing program veterans are able to progress through housing, employment, and treatment opportunities. With apartment style transitional housing veterans can receiving housing for up to 1 year); SRO's are responsible for paying a percentage of their income for rent; and through the drop-in center veterans have access to case management; showers; laundry, NA/AA meeting; job search assistance.

MEDICAL CARE:
Basic medical care services are available for Veteran Red Team Members only. Other services provided to veterans include: physical exams; immunizations; case management; medication monitoring; and assistance obtaining veteran ID cards.
