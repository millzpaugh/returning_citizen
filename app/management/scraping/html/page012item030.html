 Description: The Montgomery Avenue Women’s Center is a unique day program to the most vulnerable women in the Rockville, Maryland community. The woman are usually faced with the challenge of being homeless, but through the Women’s Centers missing the program helps to empower each individual woman with access to supportive services and lifestyle options that can enhance her safety and well-being. The program is designed to help women become self-sufficient and includes daily course work, group sessions, counseling and life skills course. The Center provides women with a service environment that is clean, healthy, caring and pleasant, using case management and supportive services.

Day activities include: substance abuse counseling; life skills; job readiness and computer classes; yoga; crocheting; jewelry making and games. Referrals are accepted from police and other agencies. Showers and laundry is available,(seven days week) for street homeless only. Services are handicap accessible and the nearest Metro Station is Twinbrook Metro station, or Metro bus line: J5.
