 Description: The Montgomery County Department of Health and Human Services offers various levels of treatment for adults with dependence on alcohol or other drugs. The Department also collaborates with providers in the community to provide continuous and comprehensive treatment for consumers with co-occurring disorders (substance abuse and mental health).

Through the Adult Addiction Services offered Montgomery county residents can receive assessments and referrals at the Center for Addiction and Mental Health Services. Additionally, through the outpatient addiction services, which are abstinence-oriented and provide individual, group and family counseling; urine monitoring; and case management participants can be back on a road to wellness.
