 Description: Our Place, DC, provides legal support services for women who are currently incarcerated or returning to the community from prison or jail. Clients are provided with direct legal assistance in obtaining medical care while incarcerated; and parole matters (hearings, parole decisions, parole grant letters. Clients may also receive legal assistance in uncontested divorces, child visitation/child custody, landlord/tenant challenges, sexual harassment claims, denial of public benefits and obtaining medical or institutional files.

HOUSING:
Through the Re-Entry Services Case Managers work individually with women while they are incarcerated to create a re-entry plan based upon the women’s self-identified needs. Case Managers meet with women once a month, or once every other month, while the women are incarcerated to begin to put their re-entry plans into action and to help connect the women to resources in DC to aid in their re-entry. Once the women are released they are expected to meet with case managers at least once a month. Case Managers assist women in obtaining identification, police clearances and birth certificates. Case managers also assist women in finding mental health services, substance abuse treatment, transitional housing, employment and legal services and other resources based upon their individual needs.

SOCIAL SERVICES:
The Employment Department at Our Place DC assists clients with finding employment, helps clients acquire job training and education, and works one on one with clients to help them attain their career goals.
