 Eligibility Criteria: DC grandparents caring for their grandchildren, great grandchildren and/or great nieces/nephews; must meet income guidelines.
 Description: The Grandparent Caregivers Program is conducted by the DC Child and Family Services Agency (CFSA). The program provides monthly financial assistance to help you care for the child related to and living with you. Although CFSA runs the program, it is only for people not involved in the child welfare system.

CFSA offers income subsidies for DC grandparents who have legal custody of their grandchildren, great grandchildren, and/or great nieces/nephews. No other relationships are eligible for program participation. Grandparents receive monthly financial assistance to help raise their grandchildren and must complete training curriculum before being eligible for benefits and they must meet income guidelines for Temporary and Needy Families (TANF). Applications are available online or by calling (202) 442-6100 to have an application mailed to you.
