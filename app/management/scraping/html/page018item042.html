MEDICAL CLINICS:
The Spanish Catholic Center offers access to primary care for adults and children. Through the programs, General Medical Services, people of all ages who are low-income and uninsured, or covered by Alliance DC are welcome. The center provides low-cost, patient-centered primary care services and specialty services on-site, including minor surgery, Gynecology, Cardiology, Pulmonology, Nephrology, Dermatology, Orthopedics, Acupuncture and referrals to other specialty services including Physical Therapy.
Additional services offered also include additional program to enhance health care, which include: chronic care initiative, diabetes focus groups, Pasos Saludables (healthy steps), medication assistance programs, ¡celbremos la vida! (cancer prevention), project wish, colorectal cancer education, diabetic eye exams and counseling services.
Appointments can be made by calling (301) 434-8985 and walk-ins are also accepted.

DENTAL CLINICS:
The Spanish Catholic Center understands the need for preventative, quality dental care in Washington, DC's Hispanic community as pervasive and largely unmet. The center operates two clinics that serve uninsured patients, providing preventive and acute dental services with the help of staff dentists, hygienists and pro bono volunteers.
Clinics provide preventive, diagnostic, emergency and restorative oral health care to adults and children who are uninsured. The dental services are available by appointment, though a limited number of emergencies are accepted from 8:30 am to 12:30 pm each day. A moderate fee is requested for any emergency services
The preventive and restorative services offered at both clinics include: Oral Exams; X-Rays; Prophylaxis; Root Canal Treatments; Fillings; and Partial Dentures.
The clinic is open to all those in need, regardless of race, age, nationality, gender, sexual orientation, residence or belief. Patients must be 18 or older, or accompanied by a legal guardian. The clinics work with low-income, uninsured families. For Maryland patients, service upon referral only.
