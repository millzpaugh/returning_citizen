 Description: Martha's Table, a non-profit organization that provides services to low-income families and individuals with food, clothing, day-care, after school and summer programs. Through these services Martha's Table strives to transform lives by engaging the community in developing sustainable solutions to poverty.

EMERGENCY FOOD
Martha’s Table offers pantry bags full of non-perishable, dry and canned foods for individuals and families in need. They offer this service on a referral basis throughout the month; please request a referral from one of the listed organizations on their website in order to pick up 1-2 pantry bags directly from Martha’s Table.

SOCIAL SERVICES
Martha's Outfitters offers a free clothing distribution program to clients referred to them by agency partners throughout the Washington, DC area. The referral program enables partner agencies to refer individuals to Martha’s Outfitters for (1) casual and work clothing, (2) interview attire, (3) housewares, and (4) linens.

EDUCATION
Daily learning activities and safe recreation programs are provided for children ages 2-17. Martha's Table also has a computer center.

VOLUNTEER OPPORTUNITIES
Court appointed community service opportunities are available. Volunteers are needed for food preparation, distribution, and tutoring.
