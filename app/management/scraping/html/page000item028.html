 Description: The Ambassador Baptist Church Food Distribution Center seeks to fulfill a Biblical mandate to feed the hungry. This is done by eliminating hunger and poverty by feeding individuals and families-including the homeless, seniors on a fixed income, and the working poor, with food donated by the Capital Area Food Bank and other agencies or individuals. This is also done through provisions of emergency assistance to the hungry by providing emergency food assistance with the help of Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA). These services are provided as needed on a 24-hour basis (by referral and appointment) and by establishing a future information and referral service to meet the identified needs of food distribution clients. Ambassador Baptist Church also provides services for spiritual counseling and employment assistance.

SOCIAL SERVICES
Ambassador Baptist Church also offers spiritual counseling and employment services. Referral from other social service agencies are preferred, but not required.

VOLUNTEERS
Ambassador Baptist Church welcomes volunteers. Court-ordered community service is also accepted.
