 Description: Head Injury Rehabilitation and Referral Services, Inc. (HIRRS) is a private, not-for-profit, 501(c)(3) agency located in Rockville, Maryland. At HIRRS, we serve individuals with various disabilities and impairments. We specialize in supporting individuals with traumatic and acquired brain injury.

HIRRS provides an individualized treatment plan developed for adults or older adolescents with acquired brain injury that is behaviorally manageable and medically stable, as well as possessing the capability of cognitive rehabilitation. Men, women and youth may receive psychological and neuro-psychological evaluations, and vocational services including job placement and supportive employment. Program accepts private insurance and referrals from Maryland Rehabilitation Services Administration (DORS) and DC Rehabilitative Services Administration (RSA).
