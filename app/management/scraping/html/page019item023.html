 Description: S.O.M.E offers several housing programs with services like emergency assistance; substance abuse rehabilitation; case management; job training and housing programs. SOME's Thea Bowman House program consists of a 10-unit apartment building and four townhouses that provide a safe and structured living environment for 14 homeless families. Families live at Thea Bowman House for two years and participate in savings and financial literacy training, as well as a life-skills program that makes it possible for them to secure long-term housing. Families are also connected to SOME's other services, such as our job training programs, group and individual counseling and our health services.
