 Description: The Riverside Outpatient Program is specifically designed to provide a continuum of treatment for adolescents and young adults who are abusing or addicted to substances and who may present with co-occurring psychiatric disorders. Treatment program approaches include: the Cannabis Youth Treatment (CYT), Motivational Enhancement Therapy (MET), and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) 12 Series. Transportation and meals are provided for all clients. The Riverside Treatment Center is an APRA Voucher Program provider.
