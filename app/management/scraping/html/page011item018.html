Lt. Joseph P. Kennedy Institute provides, 3-6 months of employment services for individuals with developmental disabilities who are in recovery from drug/alcohol use and have significant barriers to employment. To be eligible for services individual must be 30 days clean. Individuals will be required to participate in case management, which requires random drug testing; relapse prevention education; career exploration; vocational assessments (occupational guidance); job development and placement, as well as, follow-up job site monitoring.

The program also provides work preparation skills training, job development; placement and ongoing job supports; coaching and follow-up for up to 1 year; adult education; and occupational skills training. Referrals accepted from Rehabilitation Services Administration (RSA), District of Columbia Public Schools (DCPS), Department of Disability Services (DDS), Department of Rehabilitation Services (DORS (MD)).
