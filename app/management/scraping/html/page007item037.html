 Description: Hughes Memorial United Methodist Church works hard to connect with the community through ministries of service, teaching, outreach and compassion to make and grow disciples in Christ. Some of the outreach services offered include: the food pantry, clothing closet, the SHARE program and meals on wheels.

EMERGENCY FOOD
Through the SHARE program Hughes Memorial is able to provide individuals or families with a basket of groceries for much lower price than the actual retail value of the basket of food. The SHARE program is operates once monthly (usually on every 4th Saturday).Sign-up is required. Metro bus: 97 & U6.

Through the food pantry DC residents are able to obtain services most Fridays of every month, but sign-up is required for services. Additionally, through the Meals on Wheels program Hughes Memorial UMC is able to be a participant in the citywide program whereby meals are delivered daily to the sick and shut-in.

SOCIAL SERVICES
The clothes closet is available on the 2nd Saturday of every month (except July and August) from 11:00 am to 1:00 pm and there is no sign-up required for DC residents to participate in these services.
