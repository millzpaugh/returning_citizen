 Description: The Afro-American Counseling and Psychoterapy Institute provides outpatient Christian-based counseling services. Their therapeutic services include Evaluations and Diagnosis based on the DSM-IV-TR. Counseling services include individual, family, couples, pre-marital, and Christian counseling. Other counseling services include: anger management counseling, grief and bereavement counseling, PTSD intervention, domestic violence intervention, counseling for victims of crimes, addictions counseling, and services for life threatening illness. Intervention methods include: cognitive behavioral therapy, solution oriented techniques, reality therapy, eclectic, afro-cognitive-Christian centered therapy, afro-centric child therapy, and gospelized afro-cognitive techniques. This agency serves men, women, children, and those involved in the criminal justice system.
