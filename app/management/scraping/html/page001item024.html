 Description: The Burial Assistance Program for Montgomery County, MD is a program that provides assistance to families during times of bereavement. Family members of the deceased can apply for up to $1,000 toward funeral cost at the counties Emergency Assistance Service Offices.

Offices are located in Germantown (240) 777-3420, Rockville (240) 777-1100, and Silver Spring (240) 777-3075. The State of Maryland will provide an additional $5,000 toward funeral cost if the deceased was an innocent victim of the crime related to the death. Contact: MD Criminal Justice Compensation Board (410) 585-3010.

Eligibility: The deceased must have resided in Montgomery County. The funeral must be arranged with a local funeral home. Call for other eligibility requirements.
