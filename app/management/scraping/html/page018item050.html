 Description: Strive DC provides a 3-week job readiness program with 2-year post-placement support. The curriculum offered is conducted in a simulated work place that allows for group interaction and one-on-one sessions. Enrollees also learn how to take orders, accept criticism and proper usage of the telephone and computers. Sessions for programs youth, ex-offenders, non-custodial parents and TANF start on the 1st or 2nd Friday of each month and classes are held Monday through Friday, from 9am to 5pm. Graduates of the program are placed in entry level positions, paying $8.25 to $11.00 per hour. Fare cards and tokens are provided and there is a strict dress code.
