Motivated by the Gospel message of Jesus Christ, and guided by Catholic social and moral teaching, Catholic Charities strengthens the lives of all in need by giving help that empowers and hope that lasts. To this end we affirm and support the dignity of all human life, strengthen families and serve the poor and most vulnerable.

The Southeast Family Center serves as a gateway to providing help for all types of crises and needs. The Southeast Family Center, located in Ward 8, focuses on keeping families in their homes and working to find employment or other forms of assistance to treat long-term challenges.

Through the Center’s Emergency Rental Assistance program, funding to assist DC residents, who may face eviction are assisted by providing rental funds.
