Description: TERRIFIC, Inc. is a Temporary Emergency Residential Resource Institute for Families in Crisis housing and a human service organization with the primary goal of responding to families in crises. TERRIFIC, Inc. operates the following housing programs and services:

TERRIFIC INN I:
The TERRIFIC INN I is a 14-unit, subsidized housing complex for elderly and mildly disabled persons.

GRANDMA'S HOUSES:
Grandma's Hous is a four residential housing programs for abused, neglected, fragile and/or HIV infected youth and children.

THE JOSEPH AND SUSIE TATE - TERRIFIC INN II:
The Joseph and Susie Tate TERRIFIC INN II is a 10-unit, tenant-based, subsidized housing program for disabled homeless families, including persons living with HIV/AIDS.

SCATTERED SITE HOUSING:
The Scattered Site Housing program is two large, tenant-based, family homes and two apartments for families living with HIV/AIDS.

THE FRANCIOUS-XAVIER BAGNOUD HOUSE:
The Francious-Xaxier Bagnoud House is a residential housing program for abandoned, abused, neglected, fragile and HIV infected youth.

THE POTTER'S VESSEL:
The Potter's Vessel is a comprehensive case management, outreach and support services program for HIV infected/affected, socio-economically deprived, disenfranchised children and families residing in the Nation’s Capitol.
