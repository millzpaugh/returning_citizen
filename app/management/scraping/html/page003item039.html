 Description: The Department of Employment Services (DOES) helps young people in the District of Columbia make a smooth transition into the workforce. The Office of Youth Programs (OYP), as part of DOES, develops and administers the workforce development program for youths ages 14-21 years. OYP provides occupational skills training, work experience, academic enrichment and life skills training to facilitate the development of work habits and skills that are essential for success in the work place. The programs below are offered help teens transfer the valuable skills learned in the classroom to the workplace.
