REFUGEE/IMMIGRANT SERVICES
Refugee & Immigrant Service programs include: refugee resettlement for newly-arrived refugees, asylees and victims of human trafficking, refugee employment services, special social programming and immigration & citizenship counseling services. Services offered focus on immigrants from: Iraq, Sierra Leone, Eritrea, Burma, Vietnam, Somalia, Ethiopia, and Sudan. Services are offered Monday – Saturday from 9:30 am to 4:00 pm.

ADOPTION SERVICES
The adoption program has two main components: home study and post-placement visits for domestic and international adoptions. Trained professionals will help you navigate every step of the adoption process and conduct your home study and post-placement visits.
