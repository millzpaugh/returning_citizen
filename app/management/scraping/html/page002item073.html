The Center’s goal is to provide a medical home for each client. A medical home is a place where you can have a trusting relationship with a healthcare provider who knows you, a place where you can receive the care you want, when you need it. No one is turned away based on inability to pay. The Center takes many health insurances and have a sliding fee scale if you do not have insurance.
