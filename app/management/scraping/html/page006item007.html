 Description: Georgetown Ministry Center (GMC) is dedicated to guiding the chronically and most service-resistant homeless individuals towards stability and housing through street outreach, providing a safe and welcoming environment where everyone is treated with respect, and educating our community about homelessness.

HOUSING
Georgetown Ministry Center runs a small shelter during hypothermia season for men and women. Residents are invited to join the shelter at the beginning of hypothermia season, and once in, stay for the duration of the shelter. We usually do not have any beds available and typically offer spaces to clients we have a relationship with or clients referred to us by our medical team.

SOCIAL SERVICES
Guests at GMC have access to a case worker Monday-Friday. A psychiatrist and general practitioner each come once a week. Shower and laundry services, as well as mail, telephones, and computers are available seven days a week.

The nearest Metro bus lines: 32, 34, 36 & 38B and DC Circulator. They also assist residents with getting photo IDs, social security cards, and other forms of identification.
