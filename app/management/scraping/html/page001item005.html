 Description: The Behrend-Adas Israel Senior Fellowship seeks to create a unique, shared space where they can discover what it means to be human and to be part of a sacred community through the medium of Judaism.

The Behrend-Adas Israel Senior Fellowship, in partnership with the Jewish Council on the Aging, and the DCJCC, offers a hot lunch and programs for seniors each weekday. These daily programs are for D.C. residents aged 60 and over. Additionally the program provides social services; educational services; health and fitness; flu shots; field trips and referrals. Activities include weekly art; dance and body movement.

LUNCH AND FELLOWSHIP:
Seniors from the DC metro area are invited to enjoy a hot Kosher lunch. Suggested donation: $3.00, though no one will be denied a meal if s/he is unable to make a donation. Lunches are planned by a nutritionist, and are low in sodium and fat. We also offer vegetarian options.

ART, SPEAKERS, AND MORE:
We also have daily classes in art, movement, writing, plus featured speakers on nutrition, health, finance, and literature.

SHABBAT:
On Fridays, the Senior Fellowship offers a Shabbat celebration that includes live musical entertainment sponsored by the Morris Harriton Senior Program Fund.
