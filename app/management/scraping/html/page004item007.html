 Description: EFFORTS is a non-profit, tax exempt 501 (c) (3) organization and is a joint venture structured by individuals from both sides of the Criminal Justice System• EFFORTS focus is on re-integration into society; addressing the economic, educational, health and other social factors that underlie repeated behaviors. EFFORTS, helps customers with employment from local entrepreneurial alliances. EFFORTS staff train its customers in-house and send those who meet the criteria to various entrepreneurs for employment. EFFORTS provides treatment program for ex-offenders who successfully completes our Drug Treatment Program before his/her release from incarceration.

Employment For Former Offenders Receiving Treatment Services (EFFORTS) is a day program, offered Monday – Friday from 9:00 am to 5:00 pm, that offers employment readiness programs for formerly incarcerated men and women. The program includes a two-week orientation, resume preparation, “dress for success” skills training, time management skills, budgeting skills and anger management counseling. Participants will have access to employment and substance abuse referrals, Narcotics Anonymous (NA) and Alcoholics Anonymous (AA), skills training programs, basic computer training and independent living skills. To be considered for eligibility individuals must provide a current ID, Social Security Card, and current TB test results.
