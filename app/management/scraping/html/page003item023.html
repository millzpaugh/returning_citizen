Deaf-REACH’s programs include:

DEAF HORIZONS
Deaf Horizons is a day program, psychosocial clubhouse, for deaf adults who have mental illness or who are economically or educationally disadvantaged.

DEAF-REACH – adult DC residents – (202) 832-6681
Deaf-REACH is a 25-unit complex, located in Anacostia, SE Washington, DC (Otto Berg Apartments). Services include: counseling; case referral; independent living classes (including job skills and basic education); classroom for developmentally disabled (including shelter workshop). Apartment rents are Housing Choice Voucher assisted.

Community residence for deaf adults with mental illness who need to develop independent living skills. Skills include: menu and food preparation; personal hygiene; money management; using telephone and TTY system; traveling via bus and subway.
