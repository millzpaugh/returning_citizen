 Description: Trinity Arms Crossing is transitional housing provided through Community Family Life. The program is a two-year, 17-unit supportive housing program aimed at helping homeless families achieve self-sufficiency. Trinity residents are paired with a case manager that provides intake and assessment services, and matches clients with an array of supportive services including food and clothing, health and nutrition, life skills development, financial literacy and debt management, employment training and development, job placement and benefits assistance.
