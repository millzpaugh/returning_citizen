 Description: The Salvation Army's Harbor Light Center provides 136 adults with practical, positive solutions in a 24/7 residential facility of hope. The program offers an evidence-based addictions treatment curriculum in a faith based environment. Services offered at the Center include mental health and medical needs, legal issues and family dynamic issues.

One of the programs offered is a 28-day to 2 year residential treatment for persons diagnosed as drug and/or alcohol abusers. Additionally, dually-diagnosed individuals are accepted and receive mental health services at their assigned CSA. Services also include: individual and group counseling; case management; medication management; medical service 1 x week (Unity Heath Care); and drug testing. There is a bed-to-bed option for persons transitioning from CSOSA (RSC) and DC Jail (RSAT) programs.
