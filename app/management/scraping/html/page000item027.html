 Description: Allen Community Outreach (ACO) helps families living in Allen, Fairview and Lucas to rebuild their lives through the many services provided. ACO is a non-profit organization and the majority of funding for the agency comes from local, individual donations and fund raising activities. AOC focuses on rebuilding lives by providing emergency assistance for families and individuals. The Outreach Center also provides assistance to families in times of emergency, rent and utilities crisis.

ACO FOOD PANTRY
The ACO Food Pantry is available to families in need of groceries. ACO provides vouchers for families to receive food items once a month. In addition, vouchers are given to help families needing clothing. Vouchers may be spent as cash at the ACO Resale Shop located at 801 Main Street, for warm winter coats, or school clothes and shoes for children, clothes for an interview, or seasonal clothes. Vouchers must be obtained at the ACO office located at 801 E. Main Street in Allen.

MEALS ON WHEELS & MORE
ACO’s Meals on Wheels & More program serves the home-bound residents of Allen, Fairview and Lucas. The program is designed to serve seniors over the age of 60 who are home-bound or cannot prepare their own meals. A warm, nutritious lunch is delivered by a volunteer each weekday and specially packaged heat-and-eat meals are delivered prior to the holidays.

ACO’s “More” part of the program offers meal delivery to individuals younger than 60, who are homebound and need this assistance. Sometimes it is a short-term basis such as recovering from an illness or it may be a long-term service for a disability.

If you are interested in the Meals on Wheels & More program for yourself or someone you know, please call today. There is no cost involved. If you are 60 or older, contact the Collin County Committee on Aging for information on the program at 972-562-6996. After they take your general information, they will put ACO in contact with you. If you under the age of 60 and in need of meals delivery or have any questions about the program in general, contact ACO at 972-727-9131.
