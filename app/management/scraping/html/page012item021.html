 Description: The Milestone Place operates as an affiliate of Community Life Services (CFLS). The program provides affordable long-term housing for 35 formerly homeless single adults, many of whom are veterans, and/or have mental, emotional, or health challenges. Besides safe and secure housing, Milestone residents receive intensive case management, substance abuse counseling and relapse prevention support, employment counseling and access to emergency services. Through these services Milestone Residents are able to live fuller lives of self-empowerment and sustainability.
