 Description: The Phyllis Wheatley Young Women’s Christian Association (YWCA) provides provide affordable housing and programs to women in transition to prevent homelessness. Phyllis Wheatley, YWCA provides private furnished rooms, shared kitchen, bath and laundry. Housing serves employed or full-time students. Room rates are $490, $550 and $600/month, there is also a $10 membership fee, a $25 non-refundable application fee, and a $100 security deposit. The director must approve completed application. Typically, persons on probation or parole are not accepted.
