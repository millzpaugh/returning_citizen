 Description: Nursing Enterprises provides home health care for the terminally ill and in the last weeks or months of their illness. Nursing Enterprises embraces a holistic approach to health and wellness. Operating under the belief that patient education promotes a healthier society the organization is able to utilize an approach that is not only to treat the illness at hand but also to help the patient understand what factors have contributed to their illness and how to prevent further difficulties.

A specific treatment plan is developed on a case-by-case basis providing for nursing services and various therapies as needed. Or the regime prescribed by the patient's physician is followed. Once underway, Nursing Enterprises will follow up with frequent case management evaluations to check patient's progress and to adjust their treatment accordingly.
