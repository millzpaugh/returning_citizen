 Description: Project empowerment is an employment services program that is open to DC residents ages 22 -54 yrs. The program is a 3-week ($8.25 hr) paid job training and placement assistance program. Additionally, the program may provide participants with housing needs, life skills, financial management skills, as well as, mental and physical health care services. Eligibility requirements include the following: DC resident must not be receiving any other income benefits (i.e., TANF, SSI, SSDI unemployment and applicant must possess a valid government ID and Social Security Card.

Any individual who may be interested in the program must first pre-register before proceeding to enroll. Applicants can register at any if the Department of Employment Services (DOES) DC Works! Career Centers, from Mon-Fri 8:30am to 5pm.
