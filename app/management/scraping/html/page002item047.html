 Description: Clean and Sober Streets offers long term residential drug and alcohol treatment and rehabilitation services for men and women who are willing to recover in Washington, DC. Using the 12-Step philosophy, the program survives and thrives due to community support from concerned organizations and individuals seeking to make a difference in the lives of those less fortunate.

Clean and Sober Streets operates a 90-day residential treatment program; 12-step groups (daily); anger management skills; criminal justice education; life skills and 90 days of aftercare services. Residents who reside in transitional housing and are expected to work and save toward independence. Residents also have access to evening group counseling and case management.
