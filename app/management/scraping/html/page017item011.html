 Description: The Reentry Program (CTF) prepares DC Jail residents for their release to the community. Case managers prepare a “Release Plan” for each sentenced inmate and within 90-days of release, eligible inmates are transferred to a segregated Reentry Unit at CTF. Representatives from volunteer and government agencies meet with the participants and offer mentoring; counseling (substance abuse and mental health); life skills training; anger management counseling; job counseling and housing assistance. Upon release participants receive a picture ID card (good for 60 days) and referrals to community services.
