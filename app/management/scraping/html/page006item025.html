 Description: Green Pathway DC is a partnership between the Goodwill of Greater Washington and the Green Builders Council of DC, recently launched a comprehensive green job training program for DC residents in weatherization, smart meter installation, and advanced green building. Funding for the program is from part of DC’s allocation from the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009.

Green Pathway DC offers a 7-week career path skills training program in energy efficient construction and environmentally sustainable building. Classes are offered four times per year (January, April, July, and September) and in order to be eligible an applicant must have his or her GED or high school diploma and pass a drug screen. Program participants will receive a weekly transportation cost stipend.
