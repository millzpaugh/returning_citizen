 Description: C. Phillip Johnson Ministries, Inc. is a faith-based, community service organization that is holistic in its approach, and therefore, encompasses a teaching style relative to the whole man. The Ministry’s model educational program activities include General Typing and Word-Processing, Beginners and Advanced Computer Skills Training, Adult Basic Education (ABE), Pre-GED and GED Prep studies, Employ-ability Skills Training, and Drug and Alcohol Abuse Counseling, After-School Tutoring and Youth Enrichment programs, and general Pastoral Counseling. All adult evening classes are supported with childcare services for in-class students. The Center’s general adult literacy education programs are designed to meet the needs and interests of the District's adult community and are restricted to those 16 years of age and older. Courses are offered year-round and are on an Academic Schedule. C. Phillip Johnson Ministries offers a GED program. Students must pay for their own books.
