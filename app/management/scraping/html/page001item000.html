 Description: Ballou STAY's mission is to deliver a high-quality academic and career/technical program that will lead to a high school diploma or vocational certificate. Ballou STAY offers traditional and accelerated diploma programs in addition to GED Prep and External Diploma Programs; vocational programs such as automotive technology, barbering, cosmetology, Microsoft Office courses and culinary arts. The vocational program, along with sports and clubs at Ballou STAY, empowers young adults with the career skills to succeed in the real world.

Classes can be applied toward either a high school diploma or GED certificate and are offered from 10:00 am to 10:00 pm on a schedule that enables students to complete their degree in half the normal time. Vocational classes are offered from 8:45 am to 9:00 pm. Academic classes begin at 4:30 pm. Child care for children ranging from 6 weeks to 3 years old is provided from noon to 8:00 pm.
