 Description: The mission of the Washington Literacy Council (WLC) is to raise the literacy level of adults and children in the nation’s capital. WLC trains volunteers to use a structured reading program based on the latest research in language acquisition. WLC’s volunteers are the heartbeat of our program and allow us to fulfill our mission with limited staff. Volunteers come to us from many sources in the community. The Washington Literacy Council offers courses in basic and intermediate literacy. The cost is $20 per semester or $100 for the year.
