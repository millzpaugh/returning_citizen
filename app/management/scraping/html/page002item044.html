 Description: Christ House is a 24-hour medical care facility that strives to provide comprehensive and compassionate health care to sick, homeless men and women in the District of Columbia, and to assist them in addressing critical issues to help break the cycle of homelessness. Christ House takes agency/hospital referrals only. Residents must be ambulatory.

There are full-time medical staff, social workers, and case management services. There is no maximum stay. The average stay is 35 days. Christ House provides transportation and escorts to doctor appointments. There are limited resources for women.
