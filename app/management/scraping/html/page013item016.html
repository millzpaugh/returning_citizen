Description: The New Beginning Temporary Family Shelter helps homeless families in the District of Columbia transition to permanent housing by providing them with emergency shelter and the supportive services necessary to rebuild their lives. With the commitment and dedication of New Beginning staff and volunteers, and long-term partnerships with other service providers in the community, families receive supportive services that promote residential stability, increased skill level and/or income, and greater self-determination.

Supportive Services Include:

Shelter, Food, and Clothing
Individual, Group, and Family Counseling
Intensive Case Management
Parent Education
Life Skills Training
ABE/GED Instruction (Adult Basic Education/General Education Diploma)
Service Referrals
