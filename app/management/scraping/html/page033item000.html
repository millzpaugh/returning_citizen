Catholic Charities assists individuals and families in need, especially those who are most vulnerable. The services provided at the 801 East Men's Shelter and Transitional Rehabilitation Program

HOUSING
The 801 East Men's Shelter provides clothing and emergency shelter, dinner, medical care, and employment for homeless men. The men's shelter is located at 2700 Martin Luther King, Jr. Avenue, SE. This is a "low barrier" shelter. A "low barrier" shelter does not require residents to accept services as a condition of staying at the shelter.

SUBSTANCE ABUSE
The Transitional Rehabilitation Program ("TRP") has a separate 24 hour drug/alcohol recovery program. The TRP provides transportation to and from downtown. At 6am, transportation leaves 801 E to 10th & G Streets, NW; returns at 6pm. This is also a "low barrier" facility.
