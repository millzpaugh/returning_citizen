 Description: WISE is a 12-week, Workforce Development Program for out-of-school youth. Students attend work readiness training and GED preparation classes and receive case management services and follow-up job placement assistance. Enrollment is limited to DC residents and is at no cost.

Students will participate in 10 weeks of curriculum, Monday through Friday from 10:00 am to 3:00 pm. Half of the day will be spent receiving job readiness courses and the other half of the day will be spend on GED preparation. Enrollment is open to all (DC residents) and walk-in intake and orientation is held on Wednesdays at 5:00 pm. Youth may apply at the Department of Employment and will receive a weekly stipend of $100 and follow-up job placement assistance.
