 Description: The Department of Health's Court Urgent Care Clinic (CUCC) provides urgent mental health services for individuals involved in the criminal justice system. Most referrals come from Superior Court Judges, as well as other agencies located within or who provide services to defendants in the Courthouse. However, walk-ins are also welcomed. Defendants referred to the CUCC often show signs of mental illness, have been diagnosed as mentally ill, or show signs of both mental illness and substance abuse.

On-site services include: diagnostic evaluation; medication if necessary; crisis management; limited case management (up to 90 days); connection to a community based mental health services provider; referrals for substance abuse treatment and/or other social service needs. This clinic is publicly funded and currently run by Pathways to Housing DC in partnership with the DC Superior Court.
