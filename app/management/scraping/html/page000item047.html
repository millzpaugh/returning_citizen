 Description: The Assessment and Referral Center (ARC) is the single state authority for substance abuse services for the District of Columbia for residents who do not have insurance, have DC Medicaid, or whose insurance does not cover substance abuse treatment services. ARC administers the Drug Treatment Choice Program which contracts with approximately 30 treatment programs that provide services such as detoxification, outpatient, intensive outpatient, and residential drug treatment.

Adults (21+) access treatment through the Assessment and Referral Center (The ARC) at 70 N Street, NE or the satellite office at 500 Indiana Ave., NW. Youth (under age 21) access treatment from 4 different locations: Federal City Recovery Services, Hillcrest Children’s Center, Latin American Youth Center, or Riverside Treatment Center.

Youth can contact these centers directly for services. ARC also receives special funding (Access to Recovery / ATR) to provide an extended continuum of Recovery Support Services. These services include: Recovery support evaluation, Care coordination services, Recovery coaching & mentoring, Education support services (individual & group), Transportation (public only), and Environmental stability.
