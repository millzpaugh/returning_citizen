 Description: The Korean Community Services Center (KCSC) provides comprehensive social services to low to moderate income individuals and families to promote self-sufficiency and to be contributing community members. In order for the clients to access appropriate services, bi-lingual staff members assist them every step of the process from the required information gathering to application filing and follow-ups.

Services include assistance with the following:
-Social Security Benefits: Retirement (SSA), Spouse, Survivor, Disability, and Supplemental Security Income (SSI);
-Medicare Assistance: Original Medicare (Part A & B), Advantage Plan (Part C), Prescription Plan (Part D) and Extra Help;
-Medical Assistance for adults and children: Medicaid, QMB, SLMB & MCHP/FAMIS;
-SNAP/FSP (Food Stamps);
-Public Clinic Utilization and Medical Expenses;
-Government Subsidized Low-Income Family & Senior Apartments;
-Mortgage Modification Referral (MD only);
-SCSEP (Senior Community Service Employment Program);
-Information and Referral Services; and
-Translation and Interpretation Services.
