-Early intervention and preschool programs for infants and young children with and without disabilities.

-Education for school-aged children with disabilities that are too complex or severe for the public school system.

-Group homes and transitional living homes for children and youth. Group homes, independent apartments, and supervised apartments for men and women with developmental disabilities.

Greater DC Respitality gives caregivers of people with developmental disabilities a break in a local hotel, with complimentary meals and extras such as gift certificates, spa services, flowers, tickets to local events. While the caregivers are relaxing and enjoying their night off, a stipend from Greater DC Respitality covers the cost of care for the loved one with a disability by a person chosen and hired by the caregiver.
